blind tiger is a nyc-based social club that envisions a new approach to dining and going out.

about

blind tiger was created by lifelong friends who realized their hospitality experience had two serious problems. first, their favorite local bars and restaurants were struggling to connect with their patrons. and relatedly, they and their friends struggled to answer the simple question, where should we go?

member benefits

members value blind tiger's curated network of bars and restaurants, limited-time and exclusive food and drink deals, and neighborly community. whether returning to their favorite haunts or seeking somewhere new, members are rewarded for supporting their local hospitality businesses.
